MYR Group’s Commitment to Sustainable Impact

At MYR Group, we’re dedicated to coming together for the betterment of our communities, all while promoting sustainable practices. This commitment inspires us to volunteer our time and resources to support important charitable causes that help safeguard and enrich society. By providing pro bono services to local shelters, safe houses, youth centers, and other organizations, we strive to make a positive impact.

Our aim is to continually increase our positive influence within our communities. We achieve this by strengthening our current efforts, broadening our commitments, and consistently following impactful practices. Across our 13 subsidiaries, we support a variety of charitable organizations, from local initiatives to nationwide programs, including those focused on healthcare, food banks, veteran support, and child services.

Learn more about how MYR Group contributes to the well-being of the communities where we live and work.
